Here you can customize your theme.



BACKGROUNDS:
Simply drag a image in .jpg format named "system_theme".jpg into "backgrounds", examples: nes.jpg, 3do.jpg, megadrive.jpg etc..

VIDEOS:
Simply drag a video in .mp4 format named "system_theme".mp4 into "backgrounds", examples: nes.mp4, 3do.mp4, megadrive.mp4 etc..

MUSICS:
Simply drag a song in .ogg or .mp3 format named "system_theme".ogg or "system_theme".mp3 into "musics", examples: nes.ogg, 3do.mp3, megadrive.ogg etc..
